# Privacy Policy for JustLayMe

**Last Updated: November 23, 2025**

**Effective Date: November 23, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to JustLayMe ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application ("App") and related services.

This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and disclosure for users of the JustLayMe application.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Email address (required for account creation)
- Display name (optional)
- Profile picture (optional)
- Password (stored securely hashed)

**User Content**
- Chat messages and conversations with AI characters
- Custom AI characters you create
- Feedback and ratings on AI responses

**Payment Information**
- Subscription status and history
- Purchase transactions (processed by Apple; we don't store payment details)

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- App version

**Usage Information**
- Features used and actions taken
- Time spent in app
- Session duration
- Crash reports and performance data

**Technical Information**
- IP address (anonymized)
- Timezone
- Language preferences

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- Location data (GPS)
- Contact lists
- Browsing history outside our app
- Microphone or camera data (unless you explicitly use these features)
- Health or financial data

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Where We Store Data
- User data is stored on secure servers in the United States
- Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device using iOS Keychain
- Conversation history is encrypted in transit and at rest

### 3.2 Security Measures
- Industry-standard TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit
- Encrypted database storage
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication for our systems

### 3.3 Data Retention
- Account data: Retained while your account is active
- Conversation history: Retained until you delete it or your account
- Crash reports: Retained for 90 days
- Anonymized analytics: May be retained indefinitely

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access Your Data
You can view your account information and conversation history within the app at any time.

### 5.2 Export Your Data
You can export all your data (conversations, characters, account info) from the Profile section of the app.

### 5.3 Delete Your Data
You can:
- Delete individual conversations
- Clear all conversation history
- Delete your entire account (Profile → Settings → Delete Account)

### 5.4 Update Your Information
You can update your profile information at any time in the app's Profile section.

### 5.5 Opt-Out Options
- Push notifications: Manage in iOS Settings
- Analytics: Contact us to opt out
- Marketing emails: Unsubscribe link in emails

## 6. Children's Privacy

JustLayMe is intended for users aged 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 17.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 17, we will:
1. Delete that information immediately
2. Terminate the associated account

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@justlay.me.

## 11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:

- **Right to Know**: Request what personal information we collect
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out**: We do not sell personal information
- **Non-Discrimination**: We will not discriminate for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@justlay.me.

## 12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

EU/EEA residents have additional rights:

- **Right of Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Right to Rectification**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure**: Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Restrict Processing**: Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Right to Object**: Object to certain data processing
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent at any time

**Legal Basis for Processing**:
- Contract performance (providing our services)
- Legitimate interests (improving services, security)
- Consent (marketing communications)

To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@justlay.me.
